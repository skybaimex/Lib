Welcome To Phone Drive

Phone Drive is a mobile app that allows you to store, view, and manage files on your device. With Phone Drive, you can connect to your device from any Mac or PC over a Wi-Fi network and easily transfer files by simply dragging and dropping them from the Finder or Windows Explorer.


Phone Drive offers a variety of features, including a document viewer, PDF reader, music player, image viewer, text editor, and file manager. It supports a wide range of file operations, such as deleting, moving, copying, emailing, sharing, zipping, and unzipping files.


Here are some starting tips to help you get started:


File transfer between Mac or PC:

  • Ensure that your device and your Mac or PC are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. 
  • Tap on the icon in the app and select the "Wi-Fi Transfer" button to find your device's IP address.
  • Web Browser:
    • Enter the IP address displayed in the Phone Drive app into the web browser on your computer to access the file transfer interface.
  • For Mac OS X:
    • Open Finder on your Mac.
    • Select "Connect to Server" from the "Go" menu.
    • Enter the IP address of your device and click "Connect".
    • Note: If you haven't enabled authentication, simply select "Guest" and click "Connect".
  • For Windows XP:
    • Open "My Network Places" from My Computer.
    • Select "Add Network Place" and click "Next".
    • Choose "Choose another network location" and click "Next".
    • Enter the IP address of your device and click "Next".
  • For Vista / Windows 7:
    • From My Computer, click on the "Map Network Drive" toolbar button.
    • Enter the IP address of your device and click "Finish".


File operations:

  • Click on the ••• icon in the app.
  • Click on the "Select" button or long-press and select the file you want to perform operations on.
  • Tap on the icon to choose file operations such as Copy, Move, Compress, and more.


Nearby file sharing with another device:

  • Run the Phone Drive application on both devices.
  • Tap on the icon and select the "Nearby Sharing" button to search for nearby devices.
  • Select the name of the device you want to connect to and accept the connection for file transfer.

Enjoy using Phone Drive to manage and transfer your files easily!
